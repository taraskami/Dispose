Information

Users have the ability to self report the location of dumpsters anywhere. These locations are signified by a dumpster icon.

By selecting any of these locations, users will be able to see the current status of the dumpster. They can also update the current status of the dumpster if they are in the vicinity

The website defaults to the user's current location and shows dumpsters near their location. They can also search for dumpsters near any given location.
